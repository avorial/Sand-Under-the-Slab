# Bee Boat and Spirit World

After fleeing the tendril creature, the cabal is pushed into the spirit world. There they encounter a boat made of bees, not giant bees but a mass of ordinary tiny bees forming a vessel.

The bee boat carries the cabal through a symbolic and spiritual passage tied to the current mystery.

## Known Details

- The cabal enters the Umbra after a tendril attack.
- Sand becomes wet, salty, silty, or mudlike.
- The bee boat appears as a connector or conveyance.
- The experience may be tied to split avatar imagery, associations, and the cabal learning what it must understand.
- When the cabal returns, [[Tasman Quill]] indicates they have been gone much longer than expected.

## Open Questions

- Are the bees connected to Elias, the hive, or a local spirit?
- Did the bee boat choose the cabal?
- What did the spirit world want them to witness?
