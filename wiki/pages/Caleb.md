# Caleb

Caleb is one of the [[Riverbed Boys]] at [[Saltwind Line]]. He is part of the musical circle that speaks with the cabal about [[Keith's Aunt Paige]].

He is careful about what he says, especially when the conversation moves from gossip into blame, old trials, and the secrets of the [[Salton Sea Prison]].
