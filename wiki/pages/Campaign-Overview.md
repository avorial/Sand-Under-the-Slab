# Campaign Overview

Sands Under the Slab is a Mage: The Ascension chronicle set around [[Slab City]], [[Salton Sea]], and the strange magical ecology of the desert.

The player characters arrive as recent outsiders and half-initiates. Most have been in the area only a few days when a sandstorm drives them into [[The Library]], a sanctuary maintained by [[Keith's Aunt Paige]]. Her death becomes the first mystery of the chronicle and pulls the new cabal into the older politics of Slab City.

The campaign's core question is not simply who killed Paige. The deeper problem is what the mages of the desert have been hiding: something vast is imprisoned beneath or near the Salton Sea, and the prison may be poisoning the land to keep it contained.

## Starting Situation

- [[The Library]] is an anchor stone and shelter for newcomers.
- [[Keith's Aunt Paige]] is found dead, dry, hollowed out, and spiritually erased.
- A missing book and heavy permanent workings suggest Paige was pursuing dangerous knowledge.
- Local mages know rules about survival, water, secrecy, and the attention of [[The Union]].
- The desert has its own spiritual law, including the paradox spirit [[Dry Bones]].

## Main Threads

- Investigate the murder of [[Keith's Aunt Paige]].
- Understand the trapped [[Tendril Creatures]] and their connection to gold, quintessence, fertility, and sacrifice.
- Decide what to do about the [[Salton Sea Prison]].
- Learn who among the local circles cooperated with, feared, defended, or condemned Paige.
- Survive Slab City without attracting [[The Union]].
