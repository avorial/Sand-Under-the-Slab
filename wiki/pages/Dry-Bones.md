# Dry Bones

Dry Bones is a paradox or desert-death spirit associated with thirst, bones, exposure, and the desert's refusal to be made lush by careless magic.

He appears as a skeletal or deathly figure in a duster and hat. The cabal sees him after the time vision connected to [[East Jesus]] and the [[Temple of the Sun and Moon]].

## Known Behavior

- Responds to water where water should not remain.
- Fixes or removes excess water left by magical events.
- Has attacked or harmed local mages before.
- Is feared by people who understand Slab City's spiritual ecology.

## Keith's Hat

Keith receives Dry Bones' hat as a boon. While he has it, survival rolls are easier.

## Meaning

Dry Bones is not simply an enemy. He is the desert's law given personality: death by thirst, the memory of bones, and the harsh balance that keeps Slab City from becoming something it is not.
