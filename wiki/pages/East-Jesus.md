# East Jesus

East Jesus is an art-installation site in Slab City. The Gauntlet is especially low here, and the place is thick with strange resonance.

During the cabal's investigation, a broken television becomes a focal point for a vision or spirit event tied to the [[Tendril Creatures]].

## Known Events

- Ian coughs up gold and water.
- A tendril or tentacled presence manifests through the installation.
- Mark's time magic pulls the cabal into a past-and-future vision.
- The group sees conquistadors or Order of Reason figures interacting with a sedated creature at the [[Temple of the Sun and Moon]].
- The vision is followed by the appearance of [[Dry Bones]].

## Significance

East Jesus is one of the first places where the current mystery breaks through into history. It links the modern Slab City problem to older rituals involving sacrifice, gold, quintessence, and a creature that could be fed but not safely controlled.
