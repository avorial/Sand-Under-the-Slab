# Eli

Eli is the head of [[The Beat]]. He appears early as someone who can explain Slab City's rules to new arrivals and later speaks with [[Razor Tom]] at [[Music Camp]].

He is helpful but not necessarily trusted by the cabal. As the mystery deepens, the characters wonder who among the local leaders may be complicit in hiding the truth about [[Keith's Aunt Paige]] and the [[Salton Sea Prison]].
