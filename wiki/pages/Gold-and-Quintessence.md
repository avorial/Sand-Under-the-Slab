# Gold and Quintessence

Gold and quintessence appear together in the visions and aftermath of the [[Tendril Creatures]].

At [[East Jesus]], Ian coughs up gold and water after contact with the vision. In the past, sacrifice at the [[Temple of the Sun and Moon]] releases power that feeds the creature.

## Known Pattern

- Death spills quintessence.
- The creatures find this power delicious.
- Older rituals may have used sacrifice to sedate, feed, or train them.
- Overfeeding through mass killing may have broken the old control system.
- The fertility of land may be connected to how this power moves.

## Why It Matters

The gold clue ties together bodies, land, sacrifice, and ecology. The question is not only what the creatures eat, but what the world becomes when that feeding is controlled, blocked, or exploited.
