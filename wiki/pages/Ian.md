# Ian

Ian is one of the new mages gathered by the storm at [[The Library]]. He becomes closely tied to the campaign's spirit mysteries through a tome, a rock, and the name [[Gragnock Stoneheart]].

## Known Events

- Reads a spirit tome from the Library.
- Learns the name [[Gragnock Stoneheart]], a local rock spirit.
- Experiences a frightening separation or reflection involving his avatar and a rock.
- Coughs up gold and water after the [[East Jesus]] vision.
- Helps open or interact with passages into the spirit world during the confrontation with the [[Tendril Creatures]].
