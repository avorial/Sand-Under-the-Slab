# Keith

Keith is [[Keith's Aunt Paige]]'s nephew. He arrives in Slab City shortly before the chronicle begins and is drawn into the mystery when Paige's body is found inside [[The Library]].

Paige appears to have made Keith's journey to Slab City easier and may have arranged circumstances so he would arrive with people capable of solving the problem she left behind.

## Known Threads

- Paige's murder is personal for Keith.
- The Library is part inheritance, part burden.
- Local mages recognize that Paige's actions may now fall onto Keith's shoulders.
- Keith receives a survival boon connected to [[Dry Bones]] after the time vision.
