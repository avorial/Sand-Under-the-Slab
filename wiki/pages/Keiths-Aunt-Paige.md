# Keith's Aunt Paige

Paige was Keith's aunt and the keeper of [[The Library]]. She had lived in Slab City for years and gave shelter to people who needed a place to recover, learn, or hide.

She is found dead at the start of the chronicle. Her death becomes the cabal's first mystery.

## Reputation

Paige was remembered as helpful and formidable, but not harmless. People describe her as someone with strong opinions and strong passions. She had empathy for magic and spirits, but perhaps less empathy for people.

The [[Riverbed Boys]] confirm that she helped people, including [[Nina]], who stayed at the Library for four or five months when she first arrived.

## Accusations

Paige had been accused of:

- Manipulating minds.
- Traveling through time to learn secrets.
- Deleting or altering memories connected to those investigations.
- Opening spiritual gateways.
- Trying to break or bend a prison that local powers wanted left alone.

She may have been brought to trial in the local mage community. [[Mouse]] reportedly defended her.

## Death

Paige's body is dry, cold, and spiritually emptied. The death does not resemble the wet, gold-and-quintessence feedings connected to the [[Tendril Creatures]].

## Open Questions

- Who killed Paige?
- Did she free or damage part of the [[Salton Sea Prison]]?
- What books did she hide or lose?
- Was she trying to save the land, free the creatures, protect Keith, or prove she was right?
