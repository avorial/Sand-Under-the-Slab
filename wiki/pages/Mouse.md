# Mouse

Mouse is a member of [[Shattered Bottles]] and reportedly served as [[Keith's Aunt Paige]]'s defense during a local accusation or trial.

The cabal has not yet fully questioned Mouse, making them a key person of interest for understanding what Paige was accused of, who judged her, and why she was later killed.

## Questions For Mouse

- What exactly was Paige accused of?
- Who accused her?
- Who judged her?
- Why did Mouse defend her?
- Did Mouse know about the [[Salton Sea Prison]]?
