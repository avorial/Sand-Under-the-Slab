# Music Camp

Music Camp is a gathering place of roughly a few dozen people, makeshift speakers, informal stage space, and strong social magic.

The cabal arrives near sunset after the events at [[East Jesus]]. Music Camp becomes a place to recover, learn local politics, and encounter the factions that shape Slab City.

## Known Events

- Twilight performs a shared-experience DJ set using recordings and interviews.
- The performance energizes exhausted people and grants Twilight unexpected quintessence.
- A toothless, cross-eyed musician sings a mournful song about how the world can always take more.
- Eli speaks with [[Razor Tom]].
- The cabal learns more about [[Salvation Mountain]], [[The Beat]], and [[Shattered Bottles]].
