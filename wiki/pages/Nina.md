# Nina

Nina is a member of the [[Riverbed Boys]]. When she first came to Slab City, [[Keith's Aunt Paige]] let her stay at [[The Library]] for four or five months while she got her bearings.

Nina's gratitude does not mean she thinks Paige was harmless. Like the rest of the Riverbed Boys, she carries memories of Paige as both protector and danger.

## Known Threads

- Stayed at the Library when she first arrived.
- Supports some of the Riverbed Boys' guarded truth-telling.
- Goes with [[Old Man Hummingbird]] toward the [[Salton Sea]].
