# Old Man Hummingbird

Old Man Hummingbird is one of the [[Riverbed Boys]]. He carries deep spiritual knowledge and speaks through music as much as through explanation.

He recognizes the exchange of life, memory, and future in the cabal's interactions. His music can make the path of a timeline feel like a series of notes.

## Known Events

- Speaks with the cabal at [[Saltwind Line]].
- Helps reveal pieces of [[Keith's Aunt Paige]]'s history.
- Guides the cabal toward the [[Salton Sea]] with [[Nina]].
- Says that his spiritual power is not enough to contend with what is imprisoned there.
