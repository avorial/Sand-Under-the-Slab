# Open Quests

## Investigate Paige's Murder

Find who killed [[Keith's Aunt Paige]], what was taken from [[The Library]], and whether the killing was punishment, cover-up, mercy, or containment.

## Question Mouse

[[Mouse]] reportedly defended Paige during a local accusation or trial. They may know who accused her and what the community decided.

## Understand The Prison

The [[Salton Sea Prison]] is harming the land. The cabal needs to learn who built it, how it works, and what happens if it fails.

## Decide The Fate Of The Tendril Creatures

The [[Tendril Creatures]] may be dangerous victims rather than monsters. The cabal must decide whether to free, heal, train, move, bargain with, or contain them.

## Learn Salvation Mountain's Role

[[Salvation Mountain]] may be a node, route, lure, lock, or wound. The cabal needs to understand why the young creature attacked near it.

## Identify Local Complicity

Someone in Slab City may have helped [[The Union]], helped Paige, killed Paige, or hidden the truth for years.
