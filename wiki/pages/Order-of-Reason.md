# Order of Reason

The Order of Reason appears through the [[East Jesus]] time vision and the [[Temple of the Sun and Moon]].

The cabal sees conquistador or proto-Technocratic figures interacting with a system that had been used to sedate and feed a [[Tendril Creatures|tendril creature]].

## Significance

The vision suggests that outside interference, overfeeding, or the mass spilling of quintessence may have broken an older method of controlling the creature.

This historical pattern echoes the modern [[The Union|Union]] problem: a system claims to impose order, breaks something living, and leaves later people to deal with the consequences.
