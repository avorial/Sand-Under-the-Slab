# Razor Tom

Razor Tom is a member of [[Shattered Bottles]]. The cabal sees him speaking with [[Eli]] at [[Music Camp]].

He talks about [[Salvation Mountain]], the devotion that built it, and the way repeated love or hunger can make a place spiritually significant.
