# Riverbed Boys

The Riverbed Boys are a circle of mages living around [[Saltwind Line]]. They are known as allies or at least consistent friends of [[Keith's Aunt Paige]].

Their magic is expressed through music, harmony, shared attention, and memory. When they play together, the experience can carry knowledge from past to future and expose what people are giving or taking from one another.

## Known Members

- [[Caleb]]
- [[Nina]]
- [[Old Man Hummingbird]]

## What They Reveal

- [[The Library]] was an anchor stone.
- Paige helped people, including Nina.
- Paige was controversial and had been accused of dangerous magic.
- [[Mouse]] defended Paige during a local dispute or trial.
- The community knows more about the [[Salton Sea Prison]] than it openly says.

## Tone

They are not exposition machines. They are protective, saddened, musical, and reluctant to hand newcomers the kind of truth that can ruin a place.
