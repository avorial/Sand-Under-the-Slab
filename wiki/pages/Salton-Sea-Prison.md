# Salton Sea Prison

The Salton Sea Prison is the largest known mystery in the chronicle. It appears to be a magical and possibly Technocratic containment system beneath or around the [[Salton Sea]].

The prison contains one or more [[Tendril Creatures]]. It may keep them trapped by draining fertility, vitality, or possibility from the surrounding land.

## Known or Suspected Facts

- [[The Union]] is involved in creating, maintaining, hiding, or abandoning the prison.
- Pollution may be part of the working's focus.
- The prison has existed for decades, not ancient centuries.
- The land suffers because the prison continues to function.
- If the creatures are released, the land may recover, but the creatures may feed or kill.
- If the prison remains, the land likely continues to die.

## Moral Problem

The creatures are not simply villains. They may be dangerous in the way captive predators are dangerous: not malicious by human standards, but powerful, hungry, harmed, and poorly understood.

Keeping them imprisoned is cruelty and ecological ruin. Releasing them could be mass death.
