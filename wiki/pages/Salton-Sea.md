# Salton Sea

The Salton Sea is not only a polluted body of water. It is the visible face of a deeper imprisonment, warding, and ecological wound.

As the cabal approaches, the Gauntlet rises. The spiritual world tightens and resists. Old Man Hummingbird identifies this as the place where [[The Union]] left behind their problems.

## Known Signs

- Warding magic is present.
- The Gauntlet rises closer to the water.
- Pollution may be part of the prison's focus or maintenance.
- The prison has been active for decades, probably less than fifty years.
- The prison may be draining the land's fertility to keep the [[Tendril Creatures]] contained.
