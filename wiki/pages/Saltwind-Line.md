# Saltwind Line

Saltwind Line is a place of dead pads, old trailer beds, improvised hovels, and dugout spaces cut into the desert. Beneath some of the structures are cooler, moist rooms that feel almost impossible compared to the heat outside.

The cabal comes here to speak to the [[Riverbed Boys]], who were known allies of [[Keith's Aunt Paige]].

## The Cave

One underground space has a doormat-like warning: scrape your feet and leave your past behind. Inside, the air is cooler, damper, and free of blowing sand.

Music fills the place. The Riverbed Boys' playing is not just entertainment; it is a shared mind-and-memory harmony.
