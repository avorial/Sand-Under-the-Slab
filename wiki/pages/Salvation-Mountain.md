# Salvation Mountain

Salvation Mountain is a major spiritual landmark and node. [[Razor Tom]] speaks of it as a place shaped by repeated devotion, love, and the builder's overwhelming focus.

The cabal learns that Salvation Mountain may be one of the easiest ways to tap into time and place, but it is also watched, guarded, or politically sensitive.

## Known Events

- Razor Tom points the cabal toward it.
- The cabal tries to approach indirectly.
- A tendril creature attacks near the route to the mountain.
- A swarm or hive of bees becomes part of the route into the Umbra.
