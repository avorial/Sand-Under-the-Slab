# Session 00 - Prelude

The chronicle opens in June heat. The characters have been in [[Slab City]] for only a few days when a sandstorm pushes them toward [[The Library]].

Inside, they meet one another, shelter from the storm, and begin to see how strange the local community is. Bees, cobras, patched doors, improvised protections, and half-understood magical habits all frame the Library as a place that has been holding more than books.

The group discovers the body of [[Keith's Aunt Paige]]. She has been dried, hollowed, and spiritually erased rather than simply killed. The murder scene suggests missing books, heavy magic, and a deliberate attempt to leave Keith and the new cabal with a problem no one else has solved.

## Important Beats

- The characters gather at the Library during the sandstorm.
- Keith learns his aunt is dead.
- The body is unnaturally dry and spiritually absent.
- The Library itself seems protected and significant.
- The cabal begins asking who Paige was and what she was working on.
