# Session 01 - Official Game One

The first official session begins after the discovery of Paige's body. Some characters have stayed at [[The Library]], and the cabal starts the day trying to understand what the place is and how Slab City works.

Ian reads from a tome connected to Paige and learns the name [[Gragnock Stoneheart]], a local rock spirit. The name becomes a meaningful magical instrument.

The group moves through the local food and social networks, learning that Slab City has rules. [[Eli]] introduces pieces of local structure, while warnings about [[Dry Bones]], [[The Union]], and [[Salvation Mountain]] begin to surface.

## Important Beats

- The Library is confirmed as more than an ordinary building.
- Ian learns the name Gragnock Stoneheart.
- The cabal learns local rules and hears names of factions.
- The danger of Dry Bones is mentioned.
- Salvation Mountain becomes a future destination.
