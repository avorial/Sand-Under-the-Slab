# Session 02 - The Last Cup and East Jesus

The cabal visits [[The Last Cup]] and speaks with [[Tasman Quill]], an ex-Syndicate mage with a Technocratic artificial arm. Tasman explains pieces of [[The Union]] and pushes back against the idea that she is still acting as one of them.

The group then investigates [[East Jesus]]. The Gauntlet is thin there, and an art installation becomes the focus for a supernatural event. Ian coughs up gold and water. A tendril presence manifests, and time magic pulls the cabal into a vision of the past.

## Important Beats

- Tasman establishes herself as ex-Union, not safe but useful.
- The cabal learns more about what the Union represents.
- East Jesus reveals the gold-and-quintessence pattern.
- The [[Temple of the Sun and Moon]] appears in a vision.
- The cabal sees signs of the [[Tendril Creatures]].
