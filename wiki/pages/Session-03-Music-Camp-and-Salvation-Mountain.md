# Session 03 - Music Camp and Salvation Mountain

After the East Jesus vision, the cabal heads to [[Music Camp]] near sunset. Twilight performs a shared-experience set that energizes the crowd and gives him unexpected quintessence.

The cabal meets or observes [[Eli]] and [[Razor Tom]]. Razor Tom speaks about [[Salvation Mountain]] as a place of devotion and spiritual pressure.

The group tries to approach Salvation Mountain through an indirect route. A tendril creature attacks, badly injuring Twilight and forcing the group toward an Umbra passage.

## Important Beats

- Twilight's performance matters magically.
- The cabal learns more about [[The Beat]] and [[Shattered Bottles]].
- Razor Tom points toward Salvation Mountain.
- A tendril creature attacks near the route.
- The cabal opens or enters a passage into the spirit world.
