# Session 04 - Spirit World and Time Vision

Fleeing the tendril creature, the cabal enters the spirit world. The desert becomes wet, salty, and strange. A [[Bee Boat and Spirit World|boat made of bees]] carries them through the experience.

The cabal witnesses or inhabits scenes around the [[Temple of the Sun and Moon]], where a larger tendril creature is sedated and fed through ritual sacrifice. The vision suggests that older cultures had a system for handling the creatures, and that later interference may have broken it.

When the cabal returns to the current era, they learn from [[Tasman Quill]] that much more time has passed than they experienced.

## Important Beats

- The bee boat appears as a spirit conveyance.
- The cabal witnesses past sacrifices.
- The tendril creatures are shown as old, dangerous, and previously managed.
- The time difference raises the stakes of spirit-world travel.
