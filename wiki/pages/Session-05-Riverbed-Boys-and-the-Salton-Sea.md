# Session 05 - Riverbed Boys and the Salton Sea

The cabal goes to [[Saltwind Line]] to speak with the [[Riverbed Boys]], who were known allies of [[Keith's Aunt Paige]].

Caleb, Nina, and Old Man Hummingbird reveal that Paige helped people but also pushed dangerous boundaries. She was accused of manipulating minds, crossing time for secrets, erasing memories, and opening spiritual ways. [[Mouse]] reportedly defended her.

Old Man Hummingbird and Nina guide the cabal toward the [[Salton Sea]], where the Gauntlet rises and the workings of a prison become clear. The group learns that [[The Union]] left something terrible behind, and that the land itself may be paying the price.

## Important Beats

- The Riverbed Boys confirm the Library was an anchor stone.
- Nina confirms Paige once sheltered her.
- Paige's accusations and possible trial become clearer.
- The cabal approaches the Salton Sea and senses the prison.
- The moral dilemma of the [[Salton Sea Prison]] becomes the campaign's central problem.
