# Slab City Mage Law

Slab City mage law is not written as a neat code. It is carried in warnings, stories, faction pressure, spiritual consequences, and memories of what happened last time someone pushed too hard.

## Known Principles

- Do not attract [[The Union]].
- Do not create a magical crisis other people have to absorb.
- Do not casually disrupt nodes or anchor stones.
- Respect the desert and the force represented by [[Dry Bones]].
- Understand that survival sometimes matters more than correctness.
- If you break community trust, the community may judge you.

## Paige's Case

[[Keith's Aunt Paige]] may have broken or bent several of these expectations. She is accused of mind manipulation, time travel for secrets, memory deletion, and spiritual gateway work.

Her trial, defense, and death are now part of the campaign's central mystery.
