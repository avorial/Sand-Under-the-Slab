# Slab City

Slab City is the desert refuge where the chronicle begins. In June the heat sits around 110 degrees or worse, so even ordinary social movement becomes difficult and dangerous.

For mages, Slab City is both shelter and trap. The Gauntlet is strange, the desert has opinions, and the community has rules that are enforced socially, spiritually, and sometimes violently.

## Known Rules

- Do not bring [[The Union]] down on the community.
- Do not disrupt the fragile magical arrangements that keep people alive.
- Water matters, but too much standing water risks the attention of [[Dry Bones]].
- Local circles may tolerate oddity, but not carelessness.
- People who arrive broken, hunted, or pushed out often get one chance to become part of the place.

## Mood

Slab City is not a polished chantry. It is hot, improvised, protective, suspicious, and full of people who have made spiritual homes out of discarded material.
