# Tasman Quill

Tasman Quill runs [[The Last Cup]]. She is an ex-Technocrat, specifically tied to the Syndicate, and still carries obvious signs of that past, including a Union-made artificial arm.

She is wary, practical, and not interested in being treated as an active enemy. When challenged about whether she is still Technocracy, she makes the point that if she were still Union, a drone would already have solved the problem violently.

## Known Information

- Formerly connected to [[The Union]].
- Understands how the Technocracy thinks about control, systems, and compliance.
- Has survived leaving them, which is not a small thing.
- Knows enough about local danger to warn the cabal without fully adopting them.
- Tells the characters after one trip that they have been gone far longer than expected.

## Role In The Campaign

Tasman is a dangerous source: useful, compromised, defensive, and probably one of the few people in Slab City who can explain Union logic without dressing it up as either propaganda or superstition.
