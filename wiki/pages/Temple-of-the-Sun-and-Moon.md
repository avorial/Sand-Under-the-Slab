# Temple of the Sun and Moon

The Temple of the Sun and Moon is a past vision seen through the events at [[East Jesus]].

In the vision, a tendril creature is held, sedated, and ritually fed. On some days the Temple of the Sun holds the creature; on other days the Temple of the Moon does. The system suggests an old religious or magical practice built around managing a dangerous spirit-being.

## Vision Details

- Sacrifices release quintessence and gold-rich resonance.
- Order of Reason or conquistador figures interfere with or observe the practice.
- Overfeeding or mass killing may break the old control system.
- The cabal inhabits or witnesses bodies and roles from the older ritual scene.

## Campaign Significance

The temple vision proves that the creatures' relationship with people is older than Slab City and older than the modern prison. It also raises the possibility that control, feeding, and captivity have all failed before.
