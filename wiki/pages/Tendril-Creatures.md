# Tendril Creatures

The Tendril Creatures are large, tentacled or tendriled beings connected to quintessence, gold, fertility, and sacrifice.

The cabal first encounters signs of them through [[East Jesus]], then through visions of older rituals and later direct attacks near [[Salvation Mountain]].

## Known Traits

- Associated with gold and water when their resonance breaks through.
- Can feed on quintessence released by death or sacrifice.
- Were once sedated and ritually fed at the [[Temple of the Sun and Moon]].
- At least one smaller or younger creature may be loose.
- One or more larger creatures may be trapped in the [[Salton Sea Prison]].

## Not Pure Evil

The creatures are dangerous, but the transcripts frame them more like wild predators or captive whales than demons. They can kill because of what they are, not necessarily because they hate.

## Open Questions

- Were they trained by older priesthoods?
- Did the Order of Reason or conquistadors break the older control system?
- Are the modern attacks hunger, panic, instinct, or warning?
- Can a creature be taught not to feed on people?
