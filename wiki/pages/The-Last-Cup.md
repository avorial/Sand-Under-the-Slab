# The Last Cup

The Last Cup is a coffee place and social hub in Slab City, run by [[Tasman Quill]].

It is a place where tourists, drifters, and local mages can intersect without everyone admitting what they are. The cabal comes here early while trying to understand the rules of Slab City and the danger posed by [[The Union]].

## Known Events

- The cabal questions Tasman about her Technocratic past.
- Discussions here help define what the Union is and why people fear it.
- The group later returns after time in the spirit world and learns they have been gone much longer than expected.
