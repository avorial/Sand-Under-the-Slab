# The Library

The Library is the first sanctuary of the chronicle. During the opening sandstorm, the player characters gather here for shelter and discover that [[Keith's Aunt Paige]], the woman who maintained the place, has been killed.

The building is more than a collection of books. It has permanent protections and a sympathetic role in Slab City. [[Riverbed Boys]] later describe it as an anchor stone.

## Known Features

- A protected refuge for newcomers, outcasts, and people who need a place to get their bearings.
- Permanent layered magic worked into the building.
- A basement or hidden area where Paige's body is found.
- A missing or disturbed collection of books.
- A spirit tome connected to [[Gragnock Stoneheart]].

## Paige's Death

Paige's body is not simply dead. It is dry, cold, hollowed out, and spiritually absent. The manner of death does not match the wet, quintessence-rich aftermath associated with the feeding of the [[Tendril Creatures]].
