# The Union

The Union is the local name used for the Technocratic Union. In the early sessions, the cabal learns about it through [[Tasman Quill]], local warnings, and the history of the [[Salton Sea Prison]].

The Union believes it knows how reality should be organized. In Slab City terms, that means systems, control, metrics, and the crushing of independent thought.

## Known Local Role

- Formerly tied to [[Tasman Quill]].
- Feared by local mages.
- May have created or maintained the [[Salton Sea Prison]].
- May have used pollution as part of a focus for containment.
- Leaves behind problems others have to live with.

## Slab City View

Local mages do not speak of the Union as a distant ideology. They speak of it as a practical danger: drones, raids, surveillance, and the possibility that one mistake can bring the whole community down.
