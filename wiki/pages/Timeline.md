# Timeline

## Before The Campaign

- The [[Tendril Creatures]] are once managed through sacrificial rites at the [[Temple of the Sun and Moon]].
- The old system is damaged or broken after outside interference and overfeeding.
- [[The Union]] later creates or maintains the [[Salton Sea Prison]].
- [[Keith's Aunt Paige]] runs [[The Library]] and shelters newcomers.
- Paige investigates the prison, the creatures, and local secrets.
- Paige is accused of dangerous magic and defended by [[Mouse]].
- Paige is killed.

## Early Sessions

- [[Session 00 - Prelude]]: the cabal shelters in the Library and finds Paige's body.
- [[Session 01 - Official Game One]]: the cabal learns local rules and Gragnock's name.
- [[Session 02 - The Last Cup and East Jesus]]: Tasman explains the Union and East Jesus opens the vision.
- [[Session 03 - Music Camp and Salvation Mountain]]: the cabal meets faction leads and is attacked near Salvation Mountain.
- [[Session 04 - Spirit World and Time Vision]]: the bee boat and temple vision reveal older creature history.
- [[Session 05 - Riverbed Boys and the Salton Sea]]: the cabal learns more about Paige and the prison.
