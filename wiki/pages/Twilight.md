# Twilight

Twilight is one of the player characters and a natural fit for Slab City's improvised mystic culture. He acts as a drug sherpa for tourists and seekers, guiding experiences rather than merely selling substances.

## Known Style

- Uses altered states, performance, recordings, and shared experience as magical instruments.
- Connects easily with the social current of [[Music Camp]].
- Performs a DJ-style shared memory set that gives exhausted people energy and unexpectedly grants quintessence.

## Known Events

- Helps connect the new cabal socially.
- Is struck hard during the attack by a [[Tendril Creatures|tendril creature]] near [[Salvation Mountain]].
- Often becomes the person who can read or navigate the emotional current in a scene.
